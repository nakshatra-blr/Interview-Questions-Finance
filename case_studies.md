# Finance Case Studies for Interviews

## Case 1: Loan Approval
A company with Rs.5Cr revenue, D/E of 2.5, and declining profits requests a Rs.1Cr term loan. Would you approve?

**Analysis:**
- High D/E (risky)
- Declining profits (red flag)
- Recommendation: Reject or ask for collateral

## Case 2: Investment Decision
